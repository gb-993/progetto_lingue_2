    About the Project

    The first centralized digital repository for the Parametric Comparison Method.

    Our Mission

    The PCM Hub is a scientific project dedicated to the collection, storage, and analysis of syntactic data from the world's languages. Our goal is to provide linguists and researchers with a reliable dataset to study the historical relationships between languages using the Parametric Comparison Method (PCM).

    Why Syntax?

    Traditional historical linguistics often relies on lexical comparison (words). However, syntax — the abstract rules governing sentence structure — offers a deeper, more stable level of comparison. By analyzing binary parameters (e.g., "Does the adjective precede the noun?"), we can reconstruct phylogenetic trees with high precision, even for ancient divergence times.

    Open Science

    We believe in open access. The data collected in this hub is made available under the Creative Commons BY 4.0 license, fostering collaboration and transparency in the scientific community.
