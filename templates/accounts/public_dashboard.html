Welcome to the PCM Hub

The Parametric Comparison Method (PCM), Longobardi and Guardiano (2009), compares languages using universal syntactic parameters, to measure their degree of similarity, reconstruct phylogenetic relationships, and infer patterns of divergence and convergence at a cross-family level.
